What is Sanjaya?

Children today are learning not just from school, but from conversations, experiences, people, and technology.
Sanjaya – The Observer is a unique initiative supervised by India's Legendary Principals, where you silently observe and document what children learn each day — without judging or advising.

You are not a teacher

Your role transcends traditional teaching boundaries

You are not a counselor

No therapy sessions, just genuine listening

You are an ethical listener

Just like Sanjaya in the Mahabharata – quietly witnessing the journey of growth.

Your Role as an Observer

  • Conduct a short 5-minute daily phone call with your assigned student
  • Let the child speak freely – no advice, no evaluation
  • Log the child's learning, feelings, or experiences from that day
  • Submit a short structured summary – which goes to the child's parent and principal
  • Audit peer calls to maintain quality and earn insights

What You'll See

Student Profile Access

Peer Call Review

You'll be able to review peer observers' calls equal to the calls you make
